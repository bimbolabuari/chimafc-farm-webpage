About Us

Welcome to Chima FC Farms, a family-owned and operated farm dedicated to raising healthy and sustainable poultry, fish, and snails. Our story began with a passion to nourish our community with fresh, locally raised products, and a commitment to environmentally conscious farming practices. Founded in January 2013 by Dr. Favour Chima, our farm has grown into a trusted source of wholesome food, rooted in tradition and driven by innovation.

At Chima FC Farm, our mission is to provide consumers with fresh, healthy, and delicious poultry, fish, and snail products while maintaining a commitment to ethical and humane farming practices.

Our vision is to become the number one choice for locally raised, organic poultry, fish and snails for every Nigerian home. We envision a future where our agricultural practices set a gold standard for sustainability, innovation, and nourishment, inspiring healthier communities and a greener planet.

Services

Our Healthy Services

Hen rearing

We provides facilities and expertise for raising hens, including feeding, housing, and healthcare. Customers can enjoy the benefits of fresh eggs. Additionally, we often prioritize the welfare of the birds, ensuring they are kept in healthy and comfortable conditions.

Livestock Feed

We offer easy access to top-notch feed for our livestock, which is customized to meet their nutritional requirements. This feed is typically formulated considering factors such as species, age, and production objectives.

Snail Rearing

We specialize in snail rearing, offering premium snail juveniles, customized feed formulations, and training in snail husbandry techniques. Our service is designed to enhance both the growth and reproduction of our snails

Farm Treatment

Our objective is to uphold the well-being and efficiency of our livestock. This entails implementing strategies such as disease prevention and control, pest management, optimizing nutrition, ensuring environmental sustainability, and providing veterinary care for our animals.
